Do you like deers? Well I do!

Here's some facts about deers!

Did you know?

There are 55 different species of deer around the world!

The United States alone holds 7 different species of deer!

Moose are actually deer!

Note: Sadly, many deer species are declining in population, so unless listed as otherwise, assume such.

Habitat: Forest and inland wetlands
